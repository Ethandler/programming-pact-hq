Step 1: Understand the Basics

  • Notion Automations are based on Triggers and Actions.
  • Triggers include property updates, checkbox changes, and page edits.
  • Actions include adding text, assigning people, moving pages, and calling Notion AI.

Step 2: Trigger Types

  • Checkbox Trigger: When a checkbox is marked, trigger a workflow.
  • Status Changes: Trigger when status moves to "Done", "In Review", etc.
  • Field Edits: Watch for edits in select, text, or number properties.
